About Us

Established in April 2022, Mega Glass is a Limited Liability Company based in Vienna, VA. We specialize in glass and glazing contracting, fire-rated doors, and rail systems for residential and commercial projects.

Specificity of Works Performed by Mega Glass:

Glass and glazing works require:

  • Precise measurements, cutting, and fitting
  • Ability to read the design documentation (technical education is of the essence)
  • Capacity of addressing challenges such as uneven surfaces, structural considerations, and weatherproofing requirements to maintain energy efficiency and protect against external elements
  • Engineering way of thinking (analytical, problem-solving, systematic and technical approach)
